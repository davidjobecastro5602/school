Welcome to the Ministry of Tourism, Culture and Arts

The Ministry of Tourism, Culture, and the Arts (MTCA) is dedicated to promoting and developing Trinidad and Tobago's tourism sector, cultural heritage, and artistic expressions. We have also evolved over the years to address the growing importance of tourism and cultural industries in Trinidad and Tobago's economy

Here is a breif over veiw of our goals

  • Vision and Mission: Committed to positioning Trinidad and Tobago as a premier tourist destination while preserving and promoting its cultural and artistic heritage.
  • Ecotourism Initiatives: Development of the Draft Ecotourism Sub-Policy to promote sustainable tourism practices.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Provision of grants, sponsorships, and incentives to support industry stakeholders.
  • Cultural Events: Organization and support of events such as the NGC Bocas Lit Fest and the Trinidad and Tobago Gospel Music Festival.
  • Employment Opportunities: Facilitation of recruitment drives, such as those with MSC Cruises, to provide job opportunities in the tourism sector.
  • Public Consultations: Engagement with the public for feedback on policies like the Draft Ecotourism Sub-Policy.
